# Contributors

Everyone who has contributed to SIGGD Docs is listed here. Author attribution
on individual pages is handled automatically by the `git-authors` plugin, which
tracks contributions per page via git history.

---

!!! info "Your name will appear automatically"
    When you merge a pull request, your name and contribution stats are
    automatically shown at the bottom of every page you edited. This page
    serves as a high-level acknowledgment of all contributors.

## All Contributors

All contributors are credited automatically on the pages they authored.
To see who wrote a specific page, scroll to the bottom — the `git-authors`
plugin displays each contributor's name, number of lines written, and
percentage of the page they authored.

You can also browse the full contribution history on
[GitHub](https://github.com/PurdueSIGGD/SIGGD-Docs/graphs/contributors).
