# Free Assets

Curated sources for free and open-licensed game assets. Always check the
specific license for each asset before using it in a public or commercial project.

!!! warning "Always read the license"
    "Free" does not always mean unrestricted. Many assets require attribution,
    prohibit commercial use, or require sharing derivatives under the same license.
    Check before you ship.

---

## Sprites & 2D Art

### itch.io Free Game Assets

**Link:** [itch.io/game-assets/free](https://itch.io/game-assets/free)

Thousands of free 2D asset packs from indie creators. Covers tilesets,
characters, UI elements, and backgrounds. Quality varies — filter by
rating to find gems.

---

### OpenGameArt.org

**Link:** [opengameart.org](https://opengameart.org)

Community-driven repository of free 2D and 3D game assets. All assets are
clearly labeled with CC or GPL license variants. Good for game jams.

---

### Kenney Assets

**Link:** [kenney.nl/assets](https://kenney.nl/assets)

High-quality, CC0 (public domain) game asset packs. Covers everything from
UI kits to complete game asset sets. Highly recommended for prototyping.

---

## 3D Models

### Poly Haven

**Link:** [polyhaven.com](https://polyhaven.com)

CC0 3D models, textures, and HDRIs. Professional quality. Excellent for
environment assets and materials.

---

### Sketchfab (Free filter)

**Link:** [sketchfab.com/features/free-3d-models](https://sketchfab.com/features/free-3d-models)

Large library with a free filter. License varies per model — check carefully.
Good for finding unique characters and props.

---

## Fonts

### Google Fonts

**Link:** [fonts.google.com](https://fonts.google.com)

Open-source fonts with clean licensing (mostly OFL). Ideal for UI text.
Filter by category (serif, sans-serif, monospace) and style.

---

### DaFont (Free for Commercial Use filter)

**Link:** [dafont.com](https://www.dafont.com)

Use the "100% Free" filter to find fonts you can use in shipped games.
Great for display/title fonts with character.

---

## Sound Effects

### Freesound.org

**Link:** [freesound.org](https://freesound.org)

Community SFX library. Requires a free account. Licenses vary (CC0, CC BY,
CC BY-NC) — filter by license on the search page.

---

### Kenney Sound Assets

**Link:** [kenney.nl/assets?q=audio](https://kenney.nl/assets?q=audio)

CC0 sound effect packs. Well-organized by category (UI, retro, impact, etc.)
Highly recommended for game jams.

---

### ZapSplat

**Link:** [zapsplat.com](https://www.zapsplat.com)

Free with a free account (requires attribution). Large library of
professionally recorded SFX. Good for realistic audio.

---

## Music

### Free Music Archive

**Link:** [freemusicarchive.org](https://freemusicarchive.org)

Curated royalty-free music. Filter by license. Good variety of genres.

---

### OpenGameArt Music

**Link:** [opengameart.org/?q=music](https://opengameart.org/?browse-sort=views&type=music)

Game-oriented music tracks. CC licenses. Ranges from chiptune to orchestral.
